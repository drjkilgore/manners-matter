Narration Studio

Generate HeyGen avatar videos for each lesson and produce narration-manifest.json for the app. Runs in your browser; the HeyGen key stays in the Netlify function.

Before you start: set HEYGEN_API_KEY in your Netlify environment variables and redeploy. Pick a friendly stock avatar in HeyGen and copy its avatar ID and a voice ID. Do not use a real person's likeness without their consent.
